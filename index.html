Services

Hopewell Blooms is a micro flower farm that focuses on sustainabily growing a delightful mix of annuals and perennials to satisfy a variety of fresh cut flower needs. We would love to collaborate with you on your next project.

Direct to Consumer

Whether it's fresh cut flowers by the stem or a full, made-to-order bouquet, Hopewell Blooms is your local source for organically and sustainably grown flowers. Contact us for our our current inventory and pricing.

Wholesale to Florists

Take pride in offering your customers the freshest locally grown flowers from right here in your own backyard. As we like to say, our flowers are grown, not flown so they are as fresh as possible. Contact us for our current inventory.

Make & Take Bouquet Parties

Perfect for birthday parties, family get togethers, or just a fun girl's night out, our take & make bouquet parties are a great way to scratch that creative itch and come home with a beautiful bouquet of fresh cut flowers.

  • 2023

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Hopewell Farms started out with a passion for beautiful flowers and an realization that many of those beautiful flowers found locally weren't actualy grown locally. In fact, many of those flowers were flown or trucked in from far away, creating a much larger carbon footprint and a shorter lifespan in the vase. An idea was born . . .

  • Early Spring 2024

    The Seeds were Sown

    With a handful of seeds, an assortment of bulbs, a smattering of corms, and some tubers for good measure, Hopewell Farms' first crops were started. While beds were still being built in the shed, grow lights and seed trays took over the house. Out of this controlled chaos, shoots emerged and dreams started to become a reality.

  • Summer 2024

    It's Happening!

    Thankfully, the Cincinnati weather cooperated and plants made a smooth transition to the beds where they could really take off and before long we had our first customer. Through word of mouth, one customer turned into two, and then three and pretty soon the idea of a micro flower farm on our half acre lot didn't seem so far fetched.

  • 2025

    Expansion

    Looking back, there have been many lessons learned and those lessons are driving our plans for future growth in 2025 and beyond. We have new crops we want to experiment, new beds to build, and new friends to make. We hope you'll come join us and become part of the Hopewell Farms story and learn why Grown, not Flown are the right flowers for you!

Strawflower

Xerochrysum bracteatum

Strawflower, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae native to Australia. Strawflowers are an annual up to 1 m (3.3 ft) tall with green or grey leafy foliage. Golden yellow or white flower heads are produced from spring to autumn; their distinctive feature is the papery bracts that resemble petals.
